Materials & Products

Everything Your Renovation Needs,
Sourced and Supplied.

BHDR INC. supplies materials for hotel renovations — so you work with one partner for design, materials, and installation.

We supply a comprehensive range of renovation materials for hotel and motel projects of all sizes and brand affiliations. All products are sourced to meet hospitality-grade standards.

Vinyl Flooring

Commercial-grade LVT and vinyl plank flooring for guest rooms and common areas.

Floor Tiles

Porcelain and ceramic tiles for bathrooms, lobbies, and high-traffic areas.

Carpet & Soft Goods

Contract-grade carpet tiles and soft goods for guest rooms and corridors.

Windows

Energy-efficient hospitality windows — single and double-pane options.

Doors

Interior and exterior doors — hollow metal, wood, and fire-rated options.

Window Treatments

Blackout curtains, drapery, and sheer panels to brand specification.

Surrounds & Shower Pans

Tub surrounds and shower pans for guest bathroom renovation.

Shower Glass Doors

Frameless and semi-frameless shower glass door systems.

Plumbing Fixtures

Hospitality-grade faucets, showerheads, toilets, and tub fixtures.

Bathroom Accessories

Towel bars, hooks, mirrors, and bathroom hardware to brand specification.

Vanity & Kitchen Cabinets

Custom and semi-custom vanity cabinets for guest bathrooms and suites.

Quartz Countertops

Durable quartz countertops for bathrooms, kitchenettes, and wet bars.

Furniture

Contract-grade guest room and lobby furniture sourced to design specification.

Mirrors & Artwork

Hospitality mirrors, art packages, and decorative elements for all spaces.

Lamps & Lighting
