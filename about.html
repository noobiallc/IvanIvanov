About Us

Built for the Hospitality Industry

Our Story

A Renovation Company That Understands Hotels.

BHDR INC. — Best Hotels Design and Renovation — was founded with one purpose: to deliver complete renovation results for hotel and motel properties across the Southeast. We provide both the expertise and the materials.

We understand that hotel renovation requires franchise compliance, operational continuity, and an eye for what guests expect. Our team manages every phase — design, materials procurement, construction, and final installation.

Whether you are completing a brand-mandated PIP, upgrading an independent property, or planning a full renovation, BHDR INC. delivers professional results on time and on budget.
